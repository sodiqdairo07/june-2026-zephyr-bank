**Zephyr Bank H1 2026 Transaction Health Report**

Onyx Data DataDNA Challenge — June 2026
A single-page Power BI dashboard analysing Zephyr Bank's transaction data for the first half of 2026. The report focuses on completion rates, fraud patterns, channel failures, fee revenue, and customer anomalies.
Key Findings

1,500 transactions processed across Jan–May 2026, totalling £1.90M in volume
15% completion rate — only 1 in 7 transactions successfully completes
20% fraud rate — significantly above industry benchmark
Fraud spreads evenly across all merchant categories — no single segment dominates
Rajan Mehta flagged as a critical anomaly: 75 transactions, £1.12M, 100% fraud, 100% reversed
Untracked and Web channels both recorded 100% failure rates
Outbound and International Transfers drive the majority of fee revenue
Fee collection at -£0.17 — bank collecting within expected range
